Privacy Policy

This website is operated by German Simulations Pvt. Ltd. (GSIMS).
This document explains our privacy policy as required by the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
GSIMS does not collect any information that can be used to identify, locate or contact you. The only exception is stated in item 3.
GSIMS does not set any cookies in your browser, nor does it track you.
The GSIMS web server keeps copies of the server’s access logs, which contain the IP address of the device you use to access GSIMS website. GSIMS does not analyze the access log files to collect personal information. The access log files are deleted automatically after 14 days.
GSIMS will never attempt to contact you unless you initiate the contact.
